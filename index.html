Frequently Asked Questions

How does the speaker work?

Each Spartan Spirit Speaker is pre-programmed with the Pleasant Valley fight song and other school-approved audio. Simply press the button to play the music.

When will the next ordering window open?

Ordering windows are announced throughout the school year. Sign up for notifications to be the first to know when pre-orders begin.

How many speakers are made each year?

Production is limited by student manufacturing capacity. Each year's quantity depends on the team size and available time for assembly.

Are the designs really different each year?

Yes! Each school year brings new student designers who create their own interpretation of the Spartan helmet speaker, making every year's edition unique.

How do purchases support students?

Net proceeds from speaker sales go directly to supporting student clubs, performing arts programs, and STEM activities at Pleasant Valley High School.

Can I buy previous years' designs?

Previous designs will be made available. Make your choices at ordering. Order them all!
