Ordering FAQ

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and PayPal through our secure Stripe payment processor.

Can I modify my order after placing it?

Due to our manufacturing process, orders cannot be modified once payment is processed. Please review your order carefully before completing payment.

What if I can't pick up during events?

You can designate someone else to pick up for you using the "Pickup Person Name" field in your order. They'll need to provide your order number.

How will I know when my speaker is ready?

We'll send email notifications when your speaker is ready for pickup, along with information about upcoming pickup events at the school.

Can I order more than 2 speakers?

To ensure fair access, each validated email address may purchase up to 2 speakers. You're welcome to place additional orders using a different valid email address, as long as the ordering window remains open.

Is this purchase tax-deductible?

This is a fundraiser purchase. We'll provide receipt information, but please consult your tax advisor regarding deductibility.
